Common questions

Good to know.

How long does a custom print take?

Most projects ship within 5–7 days of approval. Larger or multi-part pieces can take 10–14 days. We'll always give you a realistic timeline in the quote.

Do I need a 3D model already?

Nope. Send us a sketch, photo, or written description and we'll model it for you. If you have a .stl or .obj file, even better — we can print it directly.

What materials do you use?

Plant-based PLA by default — strong, food-safe, fully compostable. We also offer PETG (more durable, dishwasher-safe). Mention preferences in the brief. Can also do other materials as requested for longer lead times.

Can I get a render before you print?

Yes — every quote includes a render preview. Nothing gets printed until you approve.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, worldwide. Shipping costs are calculated at quote time. Free shipping in the US on orders over $80. Shipping costs will vary on the size and weight of the print.
